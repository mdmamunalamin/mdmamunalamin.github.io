About Me

As a neuroscientist, I am dedicated to bridge the gap between molecular pathology and large-scale brain network organization.

My academic journey spans three continents. I received my training in Bangladesh, Germany, and Australia. This global perspective shapes my approach to translational neuroscience and my commitment to championing diversity in the scientific community.
